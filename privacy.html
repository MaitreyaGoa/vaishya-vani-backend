Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your information

Last updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

VaishyaVaniParivar.com ("we", "our", or "the platform") is operated by MHC Digital and serves the Vaishya Vani Samaj community. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store and protect the personal information you provide when using our platform.

By registering on VaishyaVaniParivar.com, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our platform.

2. Information We Collect

Registration Information

  • Full name, mobile number, and email address
  • Location: state, district, and taluka
  • Password (stored in encrypted form — we never store plain text passwords)
  • Community/family reference

Matrimony Profile (if opted in)

  • Age, gender, height, education, profession
  • Native place, current location, gotra, language preference
  • Family background and family introduction text
  • Profile photo and optional horoscope document
  • Family contact number

Business Profile (if opted in)

  • Business name, category, description, address, contact number
  • Business logo/image
  • Website and social media links

Usage Data

  • Interests sent and received between members
  • Pages visited and features used (via standard web logs)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Community directory: Your basic profile (name, location) is visible to other registered and approved members
  • Matrimony portal: Your matrimony profile is visible to other registered members (family contact is only shown if you have enabled it, or to matched users)
  • Business directory: Your business listing is publicly visible to help community members discover your business
  • Admin approval: Your registration details are reviewed by the Master Admin or Taluka Admin for your region before your profile goes live
  • Communications: We may contact you via mobile/email about your registration status, matches, or platform updates

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent or share your personal information with any third party for commercial purposes.

Your information may be shared in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Taluka Admins and the Master Admin, for the purpose of approving your registration
  • With matched matrimony members (family contact number, if enabled by you)
  • With technology service providers (Neon for database, Cloudinary for image storage, Render for hosting) who process data strictly on our behalf
  • If required by Indian law or a valid government order

5. Photo and Image Uploads

Photos you upload (profile photos, business logos, horoscope documents) are stored securely on Cloudinary's cloud storage. All uploaded images are reviewed by admins before being made visible to other users.

Matrimony profile photos are visible only to registered and approved community members. Business logos are publicly visible.

6. Data Security

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and never stored in plain text
  • All connections to our backend use HTTPS/SSL encryption
  • Database access is restricted to authorised system components only
  • Session tokens (JWT) expire after 7 days for members and 12 hours for admins

While we take strong measures to protect your data, no internet-based system is completely secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated personal data
  • Withdraw consent for specific uses (e.g. remove your matrimony profile from public listing)

To exercise these rights, contact us at the details below.

8. Children's Privacy

VaishyaVaniParivar.com is intended for adults aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has registered, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via the platform. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
